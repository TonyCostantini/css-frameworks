Koala’s fingerprints are almost identical to humans

"It is so hard to distinguish between the fingerprints of koalas and humans that even under a microscope it is hard to tell. There are even reported cases of a koala’s fingerprints confusing forensics at crime scenes" (Wolchover).

Wolchover N. Koalas Have Human-like Fingerprints. Live Science. Accessed May 11, 2021.
